ISO/IEC 27001:2022 INFORMATION SECURITY FRAMEWORK
================================================

1. INFORMATION SECURITY POLICIES
- Organization must define information security policy
- Policy must be approved by management
- Policy must be published and communicated to employees
- Policy must be reviewed at planned intervals

2. ORGANIZATION OF INFORMATION SECURITY
- Information security roles and responsibilities defined
- Segregation of duties to prevent conflicts of interest
- Management framework for control of information security
- Contact with relevant authorities maintained
- Contact with special interest groups maintained

3. HUMAN RESOURCE SECURITY
- Background verification checks on candidates for employment
- Management responsibilities for information security
- Information security awareness training provided
- Disciplinary process for security violations
- Termination or change of employment responsibilities

4. ASSET MANAGEMENT
- Inventory of information assets maintained
- Ownership assigned to assets
- Acceptable use of information assets
- Classification of information
- Labelling of information
- Handling of assets

5. ACCESS CONTROL
- Access control policy established
- Access control review process
- Registration and deregistration of users
- User access provisioning
- Management of privileged access
- Management of secret authentication information
- Review of user access rights
- Removal or adjustment of user access

6. CRYPTOGRAPHY
- Cryptographic controls policy
- Key management for cryptographic keys

7. PHYSICAL AND ENVIRONMENTAL SECURITY
- Secure areas defined
- Physical security perimeters
- Entry controls for secure areas
- Security of equipment and assets
- Secure disposal or reuse of equipment
- Clear desk and clear screen policy
- Equipment maintenance
- Removal of assets

8. OPERATIONS SECURITY
- Operating procedures and responsibilities documented
- Change management process
- Capacity management
- Separation of development, testing, and production environments
- Protection from malware
- Backup copies of information maintained
- Event logging
- Protection of log information
- Clock synchronization

9. COMMUNICATIONS SECURITY
- Network security management
- Security of network services
- Segregation of networks

10. SYSTEM ACQUISITION, DEVELOPMENT AND MAINTENANCE
- Security requirements for new systems or enhancements
- Secure development policy
- Secure development lifecycle
- Secure coding guidelines

11. SUPPLIER RELATIONSHIPS
- Supplier agreements include information security requirements
- Supplier service delivery management
- Monitoring and review of supplier services

12. INFORMATION SECURITY INCIDENT MANAGEMENT
- Management responsibilities for incidents
- Reporting information security events
- Response to information security incidents
- Learning from information security incidents

13. BUSINESS CONTINUITY MANAGEMENT
- Business continuity requirements
- Business continuity planning
- Business continuity testing

14. COMPLIANCE
- Identification of applicable legislation
- Intellectual property rights
- Protection of records
- Compliance with security policies
- Cryptographic controls compliance
