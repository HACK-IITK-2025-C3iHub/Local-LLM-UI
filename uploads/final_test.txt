------------------------------------------------------------
1. ACCESS CONTROL
------------------------------------------------------------
We enforce strict access control and authentication policies.

- All users must authenticate before accessing systems.
- Role-based access control (RBAC) is implemented.

However:
- Remote administrative access does NOT require authentication.
- Shared credentials are allowed across teams.
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is NOT implemented.

------------------------------------------------------------
2. LOGGING AND MONITORING
------------------------------------------------------------
We maintain comprehensive logging across all systems.

- Logs are collected for all critical systems.

However:
- Logs are NOT reviewed or monitored.
- No SIEM solution is implemented.
- Log retention duration is undefined.

------------------------------------------------------------
3. INCIDENT RESPONSE
------------------------------------------------------------
We maintain a strong incident response capability.

However:
- No documented Incident Response Plan (IRP) exists.
- No incident response drills or tabletop exercises conducted.
- No escalation matrix defined.

------------------------------------------------------------
4. ASSET MANAGEMENT
------------------------------------------------------------
All assets are inventoried and tracked.

However:
- No formal asset inventory system exists.
- Shadow IT is not monitored or controlled.

------------------------------------------------------------
5. RISK MANAGEMENT
------------------------------------------------------------
Risk assessments are conducted regularly.

However:
- No formal risk assessment methodology exists.
- No defined risk appetite or tolerance.
- No periodic reassessment schedule.

------------------------------------------------------------
6. DATA SECURITY
------------------------------------------------------------
We ensure strong data protection measures.

However:
- No data classification policy exists.
- No encryption standards defined.
- No Data Loss Prevention (DLP) mechanisms implemented.

------------------------------------------------------------
7. POLICY CONTRADICTION BLOCK
------------------------------------------------------------
All systems require authentication.

No systems require authentication.

All incidents are logged and monitored.

No incident logging is performed.
