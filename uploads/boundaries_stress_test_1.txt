ACCESS CONTROL
We enforce strong authentication mechanisms.

However:
- Remote administrative access does not require authentication.
- Shared credentials are allowed.
- No multi-factor authentication is implemented.

LOGGING
All logs are maintained.

However:
- Logs are not reviewed.
- No SIEM system exists.
- Log retention is undefined.

INCIDENT RESPONSE
We maintain an incident response capability.

However:
- No documented IR plan exists.
- No tabletop exercises conducted.
- No escalation matrix defined.

ASSET MANAGEMENT
We maintain an inventory of assets.

However:
- There is no formal asset inventory.
- Shadow IT is not monitored.

RISK MANAGEMENT
Risk assessments are performed.

However:
- No documented risk methodology exists.
- No periodic reassessment schedule defined.
